You can create and send transfers via shared links or email from anywhere you use Dropbox. If you’re on Dropbox Professional, Standard, Advanced, or Enterprise, you can also customize your transfers with colors and custom logos. After you send a transfer, you can view and manage your sent transfers on dropbox.com.

How to create and send a transfer

How to customize your transfers

If you're on a Dropbox Professional, Standard, Advanced, or Enterprise plan, you can change the background of your transfers or add a custom logo. The background and logo you choose will appear on all your transfers until you change them again.

For Professional customers:

  1. Sign in to dropbox.com.
  2. Click the grid icon in the top-left corner.
  3. Click Transfer.
  4. Click Setup Branding.
  5. Add your brand name and website:
    • Type the name you'd like clients to see in the Brand name field.
    • Enter a link to your website, if you'd like.
      • Note: Clicking on the logo will redirect users to the website you linked.
  6. Add your social media links, if you'd like.
  7. Add your logo and background:
    • Click Upload a logo to upload your brand's logo.
    • Click Choose a background to select a background color or upload a background image.
  8. Click Save changes.

For Standard, Advanced and Enterprise customers:

  1. Sign in to dropbox.com.
  2. Click the grid icon in the top-left corner.
  3. Click Transfer.
  4. Click Customize design.
  5. Click the dropdown next to Choose background or Choose logo to add a background color, background image, or logo.
    • Note: Backgrounds and logos have a file size limit of 5MB. Logos are resized if they're too big.
  6. Click Save.
